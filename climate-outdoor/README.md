## Gotchas

- Adafruit battery is JST 2.0, while T8 1.7.1 is a JST 1.2
- Adafruit batteries are Reversed polarity to the T8 plugs.
- CHECK YOUR POLARITY!
- I let magic smoke out with my JST 2.0 -> 1.2 converter
